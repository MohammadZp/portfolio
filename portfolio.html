About Me

Greetings! I'm Mohammad, a passionate freelance web developer with a robust background in computer science and software engineering. I earned my master's degree from the University of Tarbiat Modares, where I immersed myself in the world of technology.

With over 3 years of hands-on experience, specializing in web development at Dotin, I bring expertise in Java, the Spring framework, and Hibernate for robust back-end solutions. On the front end, I choose ReactJS to infuse creativity and functionality into my web applications.

In addition to my proficiency in web development, I've delved into the fascinating realms of machine learning and deep learning. My exploration of AI programming and computer vision using Python has equipped me with a versatile skill set, allowing me to bring innovative solutions to the table.

What truly sets me apart is not just my technical prowess but my genuine enthusiasm for creating impactful digital solutions. Whether you're looking to enhance your website or need a dedicated team member, I'm here to bring your vision to life.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and get in touch with any questions or project inquiries. Let's embark on a journey of innovation and create something extraordinary together!
